Privacy Policy

Effective Date: W.E.F 20th July 2025

At Wild by Nature, we are profoundly committed to safeguarding the privacy and personal data of our users. This Privacy Policy comprehensively outlines how we collect, use, disclose, store and protect your personal data when you interact with our website, located at www.wildbynature.co.in and all associated services, including but not limited to membership, mentorship programs, marketplace transactions, tour bookings, competitions, workshops and merchandise purchases.

By accessing, browsing, registering for an account or otherwise using any part of the Wild by Nature platform and its services, you explicitly consent to the collection, use and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not use our website or services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information from and about you, which may include:

  • 1.1. Personal Data: Full name, email address, contact number, postal address, identification proofs, payment details etc.
  • 1.2. Account Data: Username, encrypted password, profile photo, preferences.
  • 1.3. Usage Data: Device info, browser, IP, interactions, clickstream.
  • 1.4. Media Submissions: Uploaded photos, videos, assignments.
  • 1.5. Communication Data: Emails, chats, and support logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • 2.1. Service Provision: Manage accounts, bookings, purchases.
  • 2.2. Order Fulfilment: Process and deliver merchandise.
  • 2.3. Platform Improvement: Analyze usage, fix bugs, improve experience.
  • 2.4. Communication: Respond to queries, support, updates.
  • 2.5. Marketing and Promotion: Newsletters and offers (opt-in only).
  • 2.6. Security and Compliance: Fraud prevention, rule enforcement.
  • 2.7. Legal Obligations: Comply with Indian laws.

3. Sharing and Disclosure of Data

  • 3.1. Service Providers: Razorpay, Blue Dart, analytics tools etc.
  • 3.2. Mentors and Event Facilitators: For relevant activities.
  • 3.3. Legal Compliance: Court orders, safety-related concerns.

We categorically state that we do not sell, rent or lease your personal data to third parties for their independent marketing purposes.
