Karnataka, India

Rakhi Garg is a passionate wildlife photographer whose lens reflects not just the wild, but the soul behind every moment in nature. Based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, she began her journey into wildlife photography in 2021, and since then, has captured breathtaking moments across India’s most iconic jungles—from the dense forests of Kanha and Tadoba to the arid beauty of Jawai and Ranthambore. Her preferred gear includes Sony equipment, and her photography spans mammals, birds, reptiles, and mesmerizing landscapes. Her work has been featured in The Sun (US), The Daily Star (UK), and Rajasthan Patrika, earning recognition for both her technical skill and emotional storytelling. Beyond the camera, Rakhi wears many hats with grace. She’s a proud homemaker, a culinary creator known for elegant patisserie, and a nurturing mother of two. She finds joy in gardening, soulful music, and meaningful conversations with close friends. Her life is a unique blend of home, heart, and the untamed wild. As a mentor, Rakhi brings not just experience but empathy. She believes in empowering others—especially women—to find their voice, whether through art, nature, or daily life. Her philosophy is rooted in inclusion, strength, and quiet perseverance. With a global travel portfolio including Europe, New Zealand, United Kingdom, Singapore,Scotland, and Bhutan, and a rich grounding in Indian wilderness, Rakhi now mentors aspiring photographers through Wild by Nature. She leads with warmth, curiosity, and a commitment to making space for every unique story behind the lens.
