ABOUT US

Wild By Nature is a purpose-driven platform built to unite wildlife photographers, mentors, conservationists, and explorers. Born from the need for a meaningful ecosystem in India’s wildlife photography scene, our goal is to empower and connect those who capture the wild with those who protect it. Whether you’re looking to refine your skills, access reliable gear, find ethical safari operators, or share your work with the world, we’re here to help you thrive. This is more than just a platform. It’s a movement built on passion, community, and the wild itself

Vision And Mission

We envision a world where every wildlife photograph not only tells a story but also sparks action. Our mission is to bridge the gaps that hold back emerging talent, while uplifting the entire wildlife photography ecosystem in India and beyond.

From mentorship and equipment access to tours, exhibitions, and conservation-first practices, we aim to nurture photographers at all stages of their journey. Because protecting the wild starts with those who see it – frame by frame.
