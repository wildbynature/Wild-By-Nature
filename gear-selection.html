About The Course

This comprehensive course is designed for aspiring wildlife photographers who want to build a strong foundation in gear knowledge. You'll explore different types of camera bodies, lenses, tripods, and other accessories. We will break down the advantages and disadvantages of various tools and help you select the right equipment based on your field conditions, personal style, and photography goals.

You'll also gain practical insight into gear setup, handling in real field scenarios, and how to prepare for unpredictable wildlife moments. By the end of this course, you'll have clarity on the gear that suits your workflow and a deeper understanding of how to use it effectively in the wild.
