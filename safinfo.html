WILDLIFE PARKS AND RESERVES

After learning about numerous incidents and cases of cheating, from fellow wildlife enthusiasts, we decided to take a step in the matter. We did a thorough research to compile the names and details of approximately 165 wildlife parks and reserves in India and went above and beyond to uncover the official websites of these treasures, which are monitored and managed by the Government of India.

All information has been meticulously researched and verified to ensure the highest level of accuracy. If you notice any errors, please do let us know, and we will gladly update our records.
