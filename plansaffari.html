About The Course

Planning a safari is more than just a trip—it's an art that requires careful preparation and awareness. This course is designed to guide you through every essential step: from selecting the best destinations and seasons for wildlife sightings, to understanding animal behavior and safety protocols in the wild. You’ll learn how to pack effectively, choose the right gear, and work with expert guides to enhance your experience. Whether you're a first-time explorer or a seasoned traveler, this course ensures your safari is not only unforgettable but also respectful of nature and sustainable in practice.
