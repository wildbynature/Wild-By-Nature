About The Course

The Gear Setup course focuses on preparing your wildlife photography equipment for quick and efficient use in the field. You'll learn how to position your camera and lenses for fast access, master tripod settings for various terrain, and configure autofocus and custom buttons to respond instantly to wildlife action.

We’ll walk you through real-world setup demonstrations, share expert tips to avoid common gear mishandling, and show how to stay ready when that once-in-a-lifetime wildlife shot appears. This workshop is ideal for beginners and intermediate photographers looking to upgrade their gear fluency.
